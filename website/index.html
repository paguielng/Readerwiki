About Us

Reader Corp. LLC.

Reader is an innovative project developed by a team passionate about new technologies, solutions and artificial intelligence, dedicated to making reading accessible, intuitive and enjoyable for everyone. Our mission is to use the latest technological advances to help users better understand, remember and interact with the information they read, whether it is books, texts, documents or even images.

We firmly believe that reading should not be a challenge, but an enriching experience suitable for everyone. That's why we created Reader, an intelligent tool that adapts to your needs, your pace and your reading style. Whether you're a student, a professional, or simply an avid reader, Reader is designed to support you every step of the way.

With features like synchronized linear highlighting, multilingual voice reading, speech recognition for dictation, and extracting text from images, Reader pushes the boundaries of traditional reading. We integrate cutting-edge AI models to ensure a seamless, personalized experience that is accessible to everyone, including people with dyslexia or low vision.

Our vision is to democratize access to knowledge by making reading more interactive, more engaging and more effective. Join us in this project and discover how Reader can transform the way you read and learn.
